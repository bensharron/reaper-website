The Game

Reaper is a social-strategic reality game like no other. Contestants are sequestered away from reality and must not only compete and coordinate with their comrades in the house, but must contend with the fickle fate of the Reaper. Do you have the mental and physical skills and, most importantly, the luck necessary to conquer your opponents and defeat Death himself? Find out.
